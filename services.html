OUR SERVICES

ANNUAL MAINTENANCE
CONTRACT

Having your property covered under our Annual Maintenance Contracts gives you round the clock 24/7 365 days access to our team with an emergency response time maximum of 60 minutes for any maintenance issue you may face. AMC assures you complete peace of mind.

BUILDING MAINTENENCE

Our Building Maintenance Division is able to respond quickly to emergencies, work to a planned schedule of routine work, or help you refurbish or re-design your premises to ensure they continue to meet your
needs.

ELECTRICAL REPAIRS &
INSTALLATIONS

Techwin can help with all electrical repairs and can offer one-off servicing to keep your electrical system in fine working order. With proper maintenance and repairs by qualified electricians, we can reduce the risk of electric shocks or even worse - fires.

CARPENTRY & FLOORING SERVICES

We are able to carry out any new tile installation or repair for both domestic and commercial premises. We can install the tiles across all areas in your property, from kitchens, bathrooms, floor tiles in living rooms and external
tiles through to basic wall tiling and splash backs - Techwin does it all. So if you want a new installation or repair please contact us.

PLUMBING & SANITARY SERVICES

All our plumbers are fully qualified and highly experienced having spent many years perfecting their skills. We are specialists in dealing with all plumbing issues including: Burst pipes, Leaks, Pipe work repairs and maintenance, Bathroom refurbishments, Blocked baths, sinks, toilets, Water-jetting, Drain scanning, Pipe work and installations, Sewage problems etc.

PAINTING SERVICES

Our skilled and fully-trained engineers are highly experienced in all types of painting and decorating work both commercial and domestic. Painting and decorating service cover all aspects of your property. A great paint job can transform the look and feel of your home. The right paint colors can keep it looking and feeling fresh, light and spacious. Good quality exterior paint can protect your home from the severe and extreme weather conditions
